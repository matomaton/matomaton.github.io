Design Brief

Goal

What are we trying to accomplish here?

Audience

Who are we building it for? Are there personas or segmentation?

Problem Statement

As a [specific user], I want to [ability] so that [emotional benefit].

Success

How will we know we have done a good job? Metrics? Test results?

Scoping

Must have

Nice to have

Guiding Principles

If you have design principles, pick one or two to focus on.

If relevant, include one of the 10 usability heuristics.

Business Objective

How will this help the business? Ask a Product Manager, or Strategy Member, or even a VP.

Background

How did we get here? The history of the product, etc.

User Research

Personas, usability test results, marketing analysis or segmentation?
