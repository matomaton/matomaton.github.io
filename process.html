Process

Design Driven Problem Solving

I developed a flexible framework that helps product teams efficiently tackle design challenges within an agile workflow. The framework can be tailored to specific project needs and team dynamics.

Project Sizes

New Product Design, 1 Quarter

For use when a product is ill-defined, unsure of market fit, or complete product redesign.

New Feature Design, 1 Month

Explore ideas before engineering resources are committed.

Production Design, 1 Sprint

For a new or updated feature based on organizational goals and/or user feedback.

Schedule

  1. Write/Review brief, 1-2 days
  2. Sketch workshop, 1 hour meeting
  3. Low fidelity prototype first pass, 1-5 days
  4. Review / Test prototype 1-5days
  5. Visual design and copy 1-3days (if design system = true)
  6. Review final design with team/stakeholders
  7. Create notification and support materials 2 days

Design Brief

  • Aligns teams around project goals
  • Informs decision-making processes
  • Provides context for requirements and user stories
  • Collaborative effort between Product Designer, Product Owner, Researcher, and Architect
