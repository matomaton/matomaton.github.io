Process

Design Driven Problem Solving

Through my journey working with product teams of varying sizes and domains I've developed a strategic design framework that enables product teams to navigate complex design challenges efficiently within agile environments. This flexible approach has consistently demonstrated its value by providing scalable and customizable guidelines.

Project Sizes

A. New Product Design 1 Quarter

For use when a product is ill-defined, unsure of market fit, or complete product redesign

B. New Feature Design 1 Month

Explore ideas before engineering resources are committed

C. Production Design 1 Sprint

For a new or updated feature based on organizational goals and/or user feedback

Structure

  1. Write/Review brief,
  2. Sketch workshop
  3. Low fidelity prototype first pass
    • Add additional passes for A and B
  4. Review / Test prototypes
    • Add additional user research for A and B
  5. Visual design and copy (if design system = true)
  6. Review final design with team/stakeholders
    • May need additional iterations for A and B
  7. Create notification and support materials
