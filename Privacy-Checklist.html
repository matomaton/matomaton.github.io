#ask-privacy

Your Role In Enforcing Privacy

To ensure that legally and ethically, we appropriately use, secure, store, and update personal data, please ask these questions


1. Have I stored personal data in an appropriate space?

Check the Vetting Dashboard for guidance, or ask to see Legal’s Data Flow—a map of all systems used in every process involving personal data.

2. Can I use personal data this way?

Check the External Privacy Policy, a Cookie Policy, and Internal Privacy Policy.

3. Have I properly communicated a planned change?

For all changes to storage, use, security, and data retention of personal data, reach out to Legal about a Privacy Impact Assessment and plan ahead.

4. What do I do when someone asks about their data?

All requests must be handled within 10 business days - forward to PAUS to kick off the process.

5. How do I communicate with a new contact?

Draw legitimate business connections between your purpose, do not send marketing messages, and offer a decline to future communications.

6. How do I help keep data secure?

Beware of phishing scams, keep ITHAKA devices secure, and additionally protect sensitive information. Report any actual or possible breach immediately to your manager and Legal.
