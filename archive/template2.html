Redesigning the Checkout Flow

A quick one-liner that frames the project. Keep it short.

Problem

48 % of users dropped off at the payment step. We needed to find out why.

Key insights

  • Users didn’t trust the security badges placement.
  • Auto-fill failed on 60 % of devices we tested.
  • Shipping cost appeared too late in the flow.

Outcome

After the redesign, drop-off decreased to 26 % and revenue increased by 11 % in the first month.
